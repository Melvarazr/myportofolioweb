ClimED

Air Quality & Carbon Emission Monitoring App

Figma Design Project Document

Project Description

ClimED is a mobile application designed to increase public awareness on climate change and air quality issues. The app empowers users to monitor real-time air quality (AQI), track their personal carbon footprint, access educational articles, and connect with local environmental communities. ClimED aims to deliver an inclusive and intuitive experience for all user segments, from environmental enthusiasts to everyday commuters.

Key Features

Real-time Air Quality Monitoring

View up-to-date AQI and PM2.5 data for any location, with detailed explanations and health recommendations.

Carbon Emission Calculator (EcoCalc)

Track personal carbon footprint from daily activities such as traveling, shopping, and electricity usage.

GeoCarbon Insights

Explore carbon emission statistics by country and year, visualized through interactive charts.

Environmental Community Hub

Discover, join, and donate to environmental organizations. Bookmark favorite communities for future access.

Educational Articles

Access and save educational content on environmental topics, including a “Liked Articles” section.

Outsourcing Services

Integration with vendors to manage outsourcing requests.

Process Overview

1

User Research & Persona Development

Conducted interviews with 6 target users to identify pain points, motivations, and expectations regarding air quality monitoring and eco-friendly lifestyles

2

Journey Mapping & Value Proposition

Created user journey maps and value proposition canvas to define key flows and product vision

3

Information Architecture & Wireframing

Built intuitive site maps and low-fidelity wireframes to organize main features and user flows

4

High-Fidelity Prototyping

Developed interactive prototypes in Figma, focusing on accessibility, visual hierarchy, and user-centric design

5

Usability Testing & Iteration

Ran usability tests and collected System Usability Scale (SUS) feedback. Incorporated user feedback to refine navigation, clarify feature names, and improve visual clarity
