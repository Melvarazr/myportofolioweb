M-Tix: Increasing user activation rate

Developed a complete project/product management plan to increase M-Tix activation rate by analyzing user journey barriers and implementing strategic solutions to convert more app downloads into paying customers.

Background

M-Tix is Cinema 21's official mobile app for booking movie tickets online, launched in 2015 to give customers an easy way to buy tickets without waiting in line at theaters. Despite having many app downloads, the company noticed that most users weren't completing their first ticket purchase, which meant lost revenue and wasted marketing efforts.

Problem Statement

M-Tix had a big activation problem - while app downloads went up 55.4% from February to March 2024, paid users only grew 14%, with conversion rate dropping from 0.58% to 0.43%. Most users were downloading the app but never buying their first ticket.

Project Goal

Increase the number of new users who complete their first ticket purchase (activation rate) by identifying and fixing barriers in the user journey that prevent users from converting from app installers to paying customers. Overview Led a complete product management plan to increase M-Tix activation rate by finding problems in the user journey and creating simple solutions to turn more app downloads into paying customers.
