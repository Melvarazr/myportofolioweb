ZonaEBT REC Calculator: Simplifying Renewable Energy Certificate Calculation

Developed a complete product management solution to simplify Renewable Energy Certificate (REC) calculation and purchasing by translating business and user needs into a transparent, user-friendly digital experience that supports companies in achieving their sustainability and ESG goals.

Figma Design Project Document

Overview

Renewable Energy Certificates (REC) play an important role in helping companies achieve their sustainability and ESG goals. However, estimating REC requirements and understanding the certification process can be challenging due to complex calculations and limited user-friendly digital tools. Existing solutions often focus on either energy simulation or certificate purchasing, requiring users to switch between different platforms. As part of my Product Owner internship at ZonaEBT, I worked on developing a REC Calculator that simplifies REC calculation, provides transparent cost estimates, and streamlines the certificate purchasing process through a single platform.

Goals

The goal of this project was to design a simple and transparent REC Calculator that enables companies to calculate REC requirements, estimate costs, and purchase official certificates through a single platform. By simplifying the overall process, the product aims to improve user confidence and encourage greater adoption of renewable energy solutions.

Process Overview

1

Product Discovery

Defined the product opportunity by analyzing business challenges, validating assumptions through hypotheses, conducting competitor analysis, and identifying user needs through personas. These findings helped shape the product vision and determine which problems should be prioritized in the MVP

2

Product Planning

Translated research findings into a product vision, defined the MVP scope, and prioritized features based on business objectives and user value. This process ensured the first version of the product focused on solving the users' most important problems while supporting ZonaEBT's business goals

3

Requirement Definition

Created user stories and acceptance criteria to transform business and user needs into clear product requirements. These documents served as the foundation for collaboration with the design and development teams throughout the project

4

User Flow Design

Designed the end-to-end user journey, covering the complete process from entering electricity consumption data, reviewing REC calculations, selecting certificates, and completing the purchase flow. The user flow ensured every step aligned with user expectations while supporting the business process

5

Prototype Collaboration

Collaborated closely with the UI/UX Designer to refine product requirements throughout the prototyping process, ensuring the proposed solution aligned with both user needs and business objectives before implementation
